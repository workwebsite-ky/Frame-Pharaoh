Common Questions

FAQ

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4–8 weeks in advance for portraits and commercial sessions, and 6–12 months for weddings. Popular dates fill quickly, so early inquiry is encouraged.

Do you travel for shoots?

Absolutely. Frame Pharaoh is available worldwide for destination shoots. Travel fees may apply depending on location. Contact us for a custom quote.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

Standard delivery is within 14 business days of your session. Rush delivery (7 days) is available for an additional fee. Wedding galleries may take up to 21 days.

What is your cancellation policy?

Bookings require a 30% non-refundable deposit. Cancellations made 14+ days before the session receive a full refund of remaining balance. Within 14 days, 50% of the remaining balance is refundable.

Can I print and use the photos commercially?

Personal use printing rights are included in all packages. Commercial licensing (for advertising, resale, etc.) is available with the Pharaoh package or as an add-on to any package.

Do you offer videography?

Yes — cinematic videography is available as an add-on to any package, or included with the Pharaoh package. We work with a curated team of videographers who share our aesthetic vision.
